About Us

For more than 75 years Evergreen Quest Rafting has been making memories that last for a lifetime !

Our History

From humble beginings Evergreen Quest Rafting has been actively helping families make lasting memories for generations to come. We understand how important families and friends are and that is why Evergreen Quest Rafting was founded.

Since 1950 Evergreen Quest Rafting has grown into a global family, we are located in more than 15 locations around the world. Our goal is to provide you with the best experience possible.
